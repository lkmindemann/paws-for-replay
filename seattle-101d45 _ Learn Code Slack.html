User Groups

While commands like @everyone, @channel, and @here call the attention of all members in a conversation, User Groups let you choose exactly who should get your message.

Choose a group name, like @design or @marketing, then select the group members. Now, when someone in your workspace mentions the User Group, only its members will be notified. You can even add those members to channels of your choice automatically!
